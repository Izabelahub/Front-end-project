About Us

The first company which wants to share mathematical embroidery's passion!

I am Izabela Szkaradek, the owner of the company. I am fascinated by mathematical embroidery and eager to teach other passionate people. I learnt how to create cards using this technique around 10 years ago and I have been improving it since then. People any age can join us as there is nothing such as being too young or too old for this type of hobby.
