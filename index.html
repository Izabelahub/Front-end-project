What is mathematical embroidery?

Mathematical embroidery is a graphic image made in a special way with threads on thicker paper or other hard substrate. To create it, you can use ordinary threads, mouline threads or strings - preferably colored ones. Embroidery consists in threading a thread according to a fixed pattern. You can do it as a hobby or create cards for special occasions.
